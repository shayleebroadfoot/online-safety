Welcome to the Phishing Awareness Module

Phishing is a cybercrime where attackers impersonate trusted entities to trick you into sharing sensitive information. This module will teach you how to identify and avoid phishing scams.

What About Smishing and Vishing?

Phishing doesn’t just happen through emails. Learn about these other forms of phishing:

Smishing

Phishing via text messages (SMS). Attackers often send fake texts claiming to be from banks or delivery companies.

Vishing

Phishing via voice calls. Scammers impersonate bank representatives or government officials to steal information.

How to Stay Safe

  • Hover Over Links: Always check where links lead before clicking.
  • Verify Senders: Check email addresses for suspicious domains.
  • Use Multi-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of security to your accounts.
  • Be Wary of Urgency: Avoid acting on emails that pressure you to "Act Now!"
  • Report Suspicious Emails: Inform your IT team or email provider about any suspicious emails.
